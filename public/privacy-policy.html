🦁 Barcelona Zoo Guide

Privacy Policy — Last updated: January 2025

No personal data collected

Barcelona Zoo Guide ("the App") is an offline-capable interactive guide to Barcelona Zoo. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Information We Access

The App requests access to the following device features:

  • Location (GPS) — used solely to show your position on the zoo map and to draw a direct path to an animal enclosure. Your location is never stored, transmitted, or shared. It is used only in real time, within the App, on your device.

The App does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Cookies or tracking data

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party map tile services when online:

  • Google Maps (Satellite tiles) — loaded directly from Google servers when the satellite layer is active. Google Privacy Policy
  • OpenStreetMap — tiles loaded from openstreetmap.org and cached locally on your device for offline use.
  • Wikipedia — animal photos are fetched from the Wikipedia REST API. No personal data is sent. Wikimedia Privacy Policy

These services may log your IP address as part of standard server operations. The App has no control over this.

3. Data Storage

All data (map tiles, animal information) is stored locally on your device using the browser Cache API. No data is uploaded to any server. You can clear this data at any time by clearing the App's cache in your device settings.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 or under any other applicable age of digital consent.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
